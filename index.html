Moon Dogs

2011

This film was made for the 48 Hour Film Project and was required to contain the following elements:

  • Genre: Mockumentary
  • Character: Sports Trainer
  • Prop: Broom
  • Line of Dialogue: "I'd prefer not to."

Moon Dogs won no awards, but was featured in the finals screening.
